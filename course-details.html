Course Description

This course is your comprehensive guide to the world of deep learning. We start with the core concepts of neural networks, including backpropagation and activation functions. From there, you'll dive into building and training powerful models for real-world tasks. You'll work on practical projects like building a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to classify images and a Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) to generate text. The curriculum is designed to be hands-on, ensuring you not only understand the theory but can also apply it to solve complex problems.

What You'll Learn

  • Design and implement neural networks using TensorFlow and PyTorch.
  • Apply deep learning to computer vision, NLP, and time series data.
  • Master advanced architectures like CNNs, RNNs, and Transformers.
  • Optimize model performance using techniques like regularization and dropout.
  • Deploy your deep learning models to a production environment.
  • Understand the ethical implications and biases in AI systems.
